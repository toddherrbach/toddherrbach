# Private job-application site.
# Goal: stay out of search results and out of AI/LLM datasets, while still
# allowing rich link previews when the URL is texted or shared.
#
# - Out of SEARCH: handled by the <meta name="robots" content="noindex..."> tag
#   on every page. We allow normal crawling so Google/Bing can READ that tag and
#   reliably keep the site out of results.
# - Out of LLMs: AI crawlers ignore the noindex tag, so they are blocked by name below.
# - Link PREVIEWS: preview bots (iMessage/Applebot, facebookexternalhit, Twitterbot,
#   LinkedInBot, Slackbot, Discordbot) are allowed, so texted links still unfurl.
